# Intravenous 2 (2023) by Explosive Squat Games / HypeTrain Digital

* [Уровни сложности](#уровни-сложности)

## Уровни сложности

Все уровни сложности открыты изначально.

| Name      | Desc |
|-----------|------|
| Easy      | As easey as it gets. No guarantee of it being fun.        |
| Normal    | Don't fret so much over your screw-ups                    |
| Hard      | Challenge yourself.                                       |
| True      | True to the original vision of the game. Won't be easy.   |
| Throwback | Reminiscent of 90s and early 2000s classics. Not in line with the game's design, and not recommended for your first playthrough. |
| Custom    | - |

### Easy

* 10 Slo-mo charges per level.
* Infinite savefiles per level.
* Receive 50% less damage.
* Enemy reaction times are noticeably worse.
* The player makes 35% less noise.
* Armor is 50% more effective.

### Normal

* 10 Slo-mo charges per level.
* 10 savefiles per level.
* Receive 1/3 less damage.
* Enemy reaction times are noticeably worse.
* The player makes 35% less noise.
* Armor is 40% more effective.

### Hard

* 5 Slo-mo charges per level.
* Falling bodies make nose.
* 5 savefiles per level, exploration grants additional savefiles.
* Enemy reaction times are worse.
* The player makes 20% less noise.
* Armor is 20% more effective.
* Your health level influences your movement speed.

### True

* 3 Slo-mo charges per level.
* Falling bodies make nose.
* 3 savefiles per level, exploration grants additional savefiles.
* Enemies will notice missing people and patrols.
* Enemies will return fire more often.
* Everyone deals and receives 50% more damage.
* Your health level influences your movement speed.

### Throwback

* 3 Slo-mo charges per level.
* Falling bodies make nose.
* Receive 1/3 less damage.
* 3 savefiles per level, exploration grants additional savefiles.
* Enemies will notice missing people and patrols.
* Enemies will return fire more often.
* Health regeneration disabled.
* Medkits and bandages are more effecive.
* Armor is 50% more effective.
* Everyone deals and receives 25% less damage.

### Custom

Гибкая настройка сложности по следующим параметрам:

General settings

| Name | Type | Desc |
|------|------|------|
| Falling bodies make noise? | on\off | If enabled, enemies will make noise when their bodies fall to the floor. This means that even if a gunshot is not heard by a nearby ally, their body hitting the floor can be heard. |
| Health affects speed? | on\off | If enabled, the player will experience a reduction in movement speed the lower their health is. Enemies are always subject to this effect, regardless of difficulty. |
| Health regeneration? | on\off | If enabled, most of the last health can be regenerated as long as the bullet does not penetrate the player's armor vest. |
| Radios for everyone? | on\off | If enabled, every single enemy will have a radio on levels where there is at least one enemy with a radio. |
| Slo-mo charges | 1-20 | Amount of Slo-mo charges per level. |
| Medkit efficiency | 0.5-3 | Multiplier for the amount of health a medkit can restore. |
| Bandage efficiency | 0.5-3 | Multiplier for the amount of health a single bandage can restore. |

Saving

| Name | Type | Desc |
|------|------|------|
| Saves per level | Unlimited-10 | - |
| Milestone save bonus | 0-2 | Extra savefiles, usually given when reaching/completing objectives. |
| Level progress save bonus | 0-2 | Extra savefiles given for reaching various areas on game levels. |
| Unique weapon bonus | 0-2 | Extra savefiles given for picking up a unique weapon found on the level. |
| Level travel save bonus | 0-2 | Extra savefiles given for exploring the game level, regardless of whether objectives are the focus. |
| Exploration save distance | 40-100 | Required travel distance to be granted the level exploration savefiles. |
| Money pickups save bonus | 0-5 | Amount of extra savefiles the player can receive for money pickups. |
| Money pickups to save | 0-5 | Amount of money pickups the player has to grab in order to be granted a money pickup savefile bonus. |

Damage

| Name | Type | Desc |
|------|------|------|
| Global damage | 50%-200% | Influences the amount of damage everyone deals and receives. |
| Player damage (received) | 50%-200% | Influences the amount of damage the player receives. |
| Player damage (dealt) | 50%-200% | Influences the amount of damage the player deals to enemies. |
| Armor degrade speed | 50%-200% | Influences how fast an armor vest degrades when the player takes gunfire damage. |
| Armor protection | 50%-200% | Influences the effectiveness of armor damage negation. |

Enemies

| Name | Type | Desc |
|------|------|------|
| Enemy sight range | 70%-130% | - |
| Player noise | 50%-130% | Influences the amount of noise the player makes. |
| Gunfire noise | 50%-150% | Influences the amount of noise all gunfire and explosions. |
| Notice missing patrols? | on\off | Enemies will notice patrols that go missing. |
| Notice dropped weapons? | on\off | Enemies will notice weapons on the floor and become suspicious. |
| Increased return fire? | on\off | Enemies will open fire on areas they believe the player might be in. |

Randomization

| Name | Type | Desc |
|------|------|------|
| Randomize weapons | Disabled\Fair\Unfair\Very Unfair\Ridiculous | - |
| Randomize flashlight | on\off | Enables additional random flashlight to be potentially given to enemies that don't have them. At least one extra flashlight will be given. |
| Randomize expertise | Disabled\Fair\Ridiculous | - |
